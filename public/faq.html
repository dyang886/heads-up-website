Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to some of the most common questions about Heads Up.

How does Heads Up work?

Heads Up uses computer vision and AI to monitor and improve your posture by providing real-time feedback.

Is my data secure?

Yes, we prioritize user privacy and ensure that all data is processed securely and locally.

Can I customize my notifications?

Yes, our premium plans allow you to customize the frequency and content of notifications.

What platforms are supported?

Heads Up is currently available as a Chrome extension, compatible with major desktop operating systems.
